# Law on Chemicals 2025 (69/2025/QH15)

## Scope
Parent law governing chemical management in Vietnam, passed by the National Assembly. Effective Jan 1, 2026. Replaces the Law on Chemicals 2007.

## Key obligations (for chemical handlers)
1. Comply with chemical classification, labeling, and SDS requirements
2. Register hazardous chemical activities
3. Disclose hazardous chemicals in products
4. Participate in digital chemical database system

## How it applies to SDS management
This is the parent law that mandates SDS requirements. The specific SDS template and procedures are defined in implementing regulations: [[regulations/vn-circular-01-2026-tt-bct]] and Decree 26/2026/ND-CP.
