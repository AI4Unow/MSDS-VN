# Circular 01/2026/TT-BCT — MOIT Rules on Chemical Activity Management

## Scope
Issued by MOIT (signed by Vice Minister Trương Thanh Hoài) on Jan 17, 2026, effective same day. Implements Law on Chemicals 2025 (69/2025/QH15) and Decree 26/2026/ND-CP. Applies to all organizations and individuals engaged in chemical activities in Vietnam within MOIT's management scope.

## Key obligations (for chemical handlers)
1. Maintain Safety Data Sheets (Phiếu an toàn hóa chất) per Appendix I template (16 sections)
2. Classify chemicals per Appendix XV (GHS-aligned, 13 hazard categories)
3. Disclose hazardous chemicals in products per Appendix XIX (10 priority chemicals)
4. Register on national chemical database (Article 10 digital transformation mandate)
5. Obtain purchase/sale control slips for special-control chemicals (Groups 1 & 2)
6. Report chemical activities via prescribed forms (Appendices II–XIV)

## Key appendices
- **Appendix I** — SDS template (Mẫu Phiếu an toàn hóa chất): 16-section table with explanation column
- **Appendix II–XIV** — Various forms (import permits, registration, reporting)
- **Appendix XV** — Chemical classification principles (GHS-aligned)
- **Appendix XVI** — Chemical labeling requirements
- **Appendix XVII** — Chemical database update forms
- **Appendix XVIII** — Special-control chemical disclosure
- **Appendix XIX** — 10 priority hazardous chemicals in products requiring mandatory disclosure

## How it applies to SDS management
- SDS content must follow the 16-section template in Appendix I
- Sections may be reordered but all mandatory content must be present
- For products containing Appendix XIX chemicals: additional disclosure required
- Digital reporting via national chemical database (Cục Hóa chất) is mandatory
