# Decree 26/2026/ND-CP — Management of Chemical Activities

## Scope
Government decree implementing Law on Chemicals 2025 (69/2025/QH15). Replaces Decree 113/2017/ND-CP and its amendment Decree 82/2022/ND-CP.

## Key obligations (for chemical handlers)
1. Register chemical business activities with MOIT
2. Comply with SDS and labeling requirements per implementing circulars
3. Report hazardous chemicals in products/goods
4. Maintain chemical safety records

## How it applies to SDS management
This decree establishes the legal framework that [[regulations/vn-circular-01-2026-tt-bct]] implements at the operational level (templates, forms, procedures).
